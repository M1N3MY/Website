About

Welcome to my website! My name is Sarah. Recently, I've graduated from UMBC with a degree in information systems.

This website is a mix between a portfolio and personal website. It's heavily inspired by the indie web movement and websites from Neocities. I've been meaning to make a website for awhile now, but never had the passion to make one. There is no definite plan for it as of now, but I know I want to add my projects and art in here. While also personalizing this website and making it a fun place to visit.

For anyone wondering why the theme is black and white, when I draw I tend to not color in my artwork, so I thought it would be fun to keep the site black and white. Also, it's a looot easier to decide on colors Might change in the future.
